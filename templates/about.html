About Ribbit

Ribbit is a simple yet powerful URL shortener designed to make sharing long URLs more convenient and trackable. Built with modern web technologies, Ribbit offers fast and reliable service for both personal and professional use.

Whether you're looking to clean up links for social media sharing or need a way to track how your links perform, Ribbit has you covered. Our easy-to-use platform ensures that your links are both manageable and monitorable.
